Notes: Checkout Page

Purpose: To collect all necessary information to complete the transaction.

  • A two-column layout is common, with forms on the left and a persistent order summary on the right.
  • Forms: Standard input fields for address and payment. Minimal fields are best to reduce friction.
  • Summary: The order summary should always be visible so users can confirm details before paying.
  • CTA: The final "Place Order" button should be unambiguous and clearly marked.
  • A progress bar (e.g., Delivery -> Payment -> Confirm) could be added at the top.
